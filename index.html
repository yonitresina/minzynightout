All About Minzy

Minzy 공민지

Minzy, of the legendary K-POP group 2NE1 member.

She rose to fame after debuting as the youngest member of the girl group 2NE1. Besides music activities, Minzy also actively participates in variety shows such as "Unnie's Slam Dunk" in 2016, which made her a member of the project girl group Unnies.

On April 5, 2016, Minzy announced her departure from 2NE1 and its management company, YG entertainment.

After leaving 2NE1, Minzy focused on solo activities and eventually made a solo debut with her first mini album. It was also during this time that she joined forces with DME WORLD in Japan who then signed her as an official artist to their company.

Through the years she remained present in 2NE1's post disbandment gatherings and even made a guest appearance at the 2022 Coachella with other 3 members.

In July 2024, fans were in for a surprise when YG Entertainment announced 2NE1's official reunion.

Currently she is doing a world tour with both her band 2NE1 and also as a solo artist with DME WORLD.
